About Rewardive

Empowering smart spending through intelligent offer discovery

Our Mission

At Rewardive, we believe that managing and maximizing credit card and bank offers shouldn't be complicated. Every day, millions of cardholders miss out on valuable discounts, cashback opportunities, and exclusive deals simply because they're not aware of them or can't easily find relevant offers for their cards.

We're on a mission to change that. Rewardive brings together all credit card, debit card, and bank-specific offers from major financial institutions and card networks into one intuitive platform. Whether it's a Visa promotion, a Mastercard exclusive, or a special bank offer from HDFC, ICICI, SBI, or Axis Bank, we make sure you never miss an opportunity to save.

Our Story

Rewardive was born out of a simple frustration: missing out on great deals because we didn't know they existed. As consumers with multiple credit and debit cards from different banks, we found it overwhelming to keep track of all the offers available to us.

Bank websites, card network portals, promotional emails - offers were scattered everywhere, and most of them went unused. We realized that millions of people face the same challenge every day, losing out on potential savings and benefits they're entitled to.

That's why we built Rewardive - a single platform that brings all these offers together, makes them easy to discover, and helps you make the most of every card in your wallet. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of users who trust us to help them save money and maximize their rewards.
