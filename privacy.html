Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Rewardive. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Rewardive mobile application ("App") collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Important: The Rewardive app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter remains on your device only.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device to provide its services. This data never leaves your device:

  • Card Information: Card type, bank name, card network (Visa, Mastercard, etc.), and optionally the last 4 digits for your reference
  • Preferences: Your notification preferences and app settings
  • Saved Offers: Offers you bookmark or save for later
  • App Cache: Temporary data to improve app performance

Note: We NEVER collect or store full card numbers, CVV codes, PINs, passwords, or any sensitive financial information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

The Rewardive app does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Your contacts or call logs
  • Photos, files, or media from your device
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Any data that could identify you personally

How We Use Your Information

All data entered into the App is processed and stored locally on your device. We use this information solely to:

  • Display relevant credit card and bank offers based on your cards
  • Filter and personalize offer recommendations
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Allow you to bookmark offers for future reference
  • Provide app functionality offline

Since your data never leaves your device, we cannot access, view, or use your information in any way.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored securely on your device using Android's built-in secure storage mechanisms:

  • Data is stored in the app's private storage area
  • Other apps cannot access your Rewardive data
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can manually clear app data anytime from Android settings

Data Sharing and Transmission

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties.

The App operates entirely offline for your personal data. Offer information is retrieved from public sources (bank websites, card network portals) but your card details are never sent to these sources or any external servers.

Internet Access

The App requires internet access only to:

  • Fetch publicly available credit card and bank offers
  • Update the offers database
  • Load merchant logos and images

Your personal data and card information are NEVER transmitted over the internet.

App Permissions

The Rewardive app may request the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch offer information from public sources
  • Network State: To check if you're online before attempting to update offers
  • Notifications (Optional): To send you alerts about new relevant offers (you can disable this)

The app does NOT request or use permissions for camera, contacts, location, microphone, phone, SMS, or storage access.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools that collect user data
  • Social media SDKs that track users

Third-Party Links

The App may display links to bank websites, merchant websites, or card network portals. These are external websites not controlled by us. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information to these sites.

Your Rights and Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete Your Data: Uninstall the app or clear app data from Android settings
  • Export Your Data: Currently not applicable as data stays on your device
  • Control Notifications: Disable notifications in app settings or Android settings
  • No Account Required: The app doesn't require registration or login

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users who are 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. Since the app doesn't collect personal information, there is no risk of inadvertently collecting children's data.

Data Breach

Since we do not collect or store any user data on our servers, there is no risk of a data breach on our end. Your data remains secure on your device and is protected by your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Google Play Store privacy requirements
  • Android privacy guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Rewardive app, please contact us:

  • Email: support@rewardive.app
  • Privacy Inquiries: privacy@rewardive.app

Privacy-First Approach: Rewardive is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We believe you should have complete control over your data. That's why we don't collect, transmit, or store your personal information on any external servers. Your data stays with you, on your device.
